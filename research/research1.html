Neural Author Name Dismabiguator

In this research project, we explore a new method for disambiguating author names utilizng a simalarity learning frameowork. Author name disambiguation (AND) is crucial for accurately identifying unique authors in digital libraries, improving collaboration tools, and metascience studies.
