Research Update 1: AI for Author Name Disambiguation

In this research project, we explore new AI methods to disambiguate author names in large-scale academic databases. This work is crucial for accurately identifying unique authors in digital libraries, improving search results and citation records.

Figure 1: Visualization of name disambiguation in a network of authors.

Using techniques such as deep learning, clustering, and natural language processing, we aim to reduce errors and enhance the overall performance of author identification. Preliminary results show a significant improvement in accuracy when compared to traditional methods.
